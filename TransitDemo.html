Census2020 Transit Service Areas

Census data helps to inform where new bus routes should go!

Thanks to insight from Pierce Transit and King County Metro, we know that census data is used to find areas where residents might benefit from a new bus route. Census data helps to find areas with high a higher liklihood of benefiting from public transit access. Specific details that they look for inclide (but are not limited to):

  • Senior Citizens
  • Youth
  • Disabled Persons
  • Household Vehicle Access
  • Low Current Transit Usage

Finding New Service Areas

This map displays layers of census data. Turn the layers on to find areas where all demographic details intersect. These areas might be ready for new transit routes!



What if Someone Fills out the Census Wrong?

If only 8% of people living in a specific census block incorrectly listed their vehicle access, the map looks much different. Less than 100 people didn't report or incorrectly reported their car ownership status, and now the bus might not come to this South Hill neighborhood.
